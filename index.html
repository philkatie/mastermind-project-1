Album Vault


To play, select your guesses from the album vault at the bottom of the game board.

Once you're satisfied with your guess, hit the 'Submit Guess' button.

After you've guessed, the pegs on the right will reveal how you did! A green peg means that you have a guess in the correct spot. A blue peg means that you have a correct guess in the wrong spot. A white peg indicates that you have a wrong guess. The pegs do not correlate to any specific guess.

The secret code will have no duplicates.

You have 8 guesses to open the Vault and win back the Masters!
